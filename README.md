**YouTube Series Retention & Geography Forecast:-**
This project dives deep into analyzing how YouTube series are performing—tracking audience retention, engagement trends, and where viewers are tuning in from. It’s all about understanding what's driving growth and figuring out how to shape content strategies for the future.

**Key Insight:-**
- Series C consistently keeps over 50% of viewers engaged, making it the top performer in retention.
- The Philippines stands out as the most recurring growth market across all series.
- There’s a clear link: higher engagement rates often lead to better retention.
- Our forecast suggests Series A could hit around 2.1 million views per episode by 2029.
- The audience is expanding rapidly across Southeast Asia and Brazil.

**Why it matters for the business:-**
This analysis helps us pinpoint what types of content really resonate, so we can fine-tune episode length and storytelling techniques. It also guides us in focusing on markets with the most growth potential, improving our visibility on YouTube’s algorithm, and making smarter decisions about where to invest marketing efforts regionally.
